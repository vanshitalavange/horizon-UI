Avatars

The avatar component is typically used to display user profile pictures. Horizon has different sizes of avatars available starting from extra-small to extra-large

Round avatars

Round avatars are typically used to display circular profile pictures 

             
                <!-- extra-small avatar -->
                <img class="avatar-circular avatar-xs" src="replace with your url" alt="avatar-1"/>
                <!-- small avatar -->
                <img class="avatar-circular avatar-sm" src="replace with your url" alt="avatar-2"/>
                <!-- medium avatar -->
                <img class="avatar-circular avatar-md" src="replace with your url" alt="avatar-3"/>
                <!-- large avatar -->
                 <img class="avatar-circular avatar-lg" src="replace with your url" alt="avatar-4"/>
                <!-- extra-large avatar -->
                <img class="avatar-circular avatar-xl" src="replace with your url alt="avatar-5"/>
