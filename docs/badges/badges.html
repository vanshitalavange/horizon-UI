Badges

Badges are small status descriptors. They can be used to represent the notifications or status of a person

Icon with badge

An icon badge is a small circle containing number which is displayed either on right or left of the icon. These are commonly called as notification badges

email 4
notifications 8 
           
            <!-- Icon with a right badge -->
            <div class="badge">
                <span class="material-icons-round badge-icon">
                    email
                    </span>
                <span class="badge-counter badge-round badge-counter-right">4</span>
            </div>
            <!-- Icon with a left badge -->
            <div class="badge">
                <span class="material-icons-round badge-icon">
                    notifications
                    </span>
                <span class="badge-counter badge-round badge-counter-left">8</span>
            </div>
